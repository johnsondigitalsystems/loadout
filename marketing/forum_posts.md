# LoadOut — Forum Launch Posts

Four ~200-word variants of the launch announcement, each tuned to its
forum's voice. Cross-posting the same boilerplate is the fastest way
to get downvoted across all four. Don't.

A few rules that apply to every variant:

- Disclose that you're the developer in the first or second sentence.
  Reddit, Sniper's Hide, and AccurateShooter all enforce this and
  remove posts that don't.
- No engagement bait, no "what do you think?" sign-offs, no edits-as-
  bumps.
- Lead with one concrete, audience-specific thing the app does. Save
  the feature list for the website.
- Keep the link unobtrusive. One link at the end is plenty.
